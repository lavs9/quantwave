# Donchian Channels

Donchian Channels are volatility indicators formed by taking the highest high and the lowest low of the last N periods.

## Parameters

- `period` (default: 20): Channel period

## Formula


\[
UC = \max(H_{t-n \dots t}) \\ LC = \min(L_{t-n \dots t})
\]
